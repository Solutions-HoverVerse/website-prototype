SurfaceWare™ · the engine

Deterministic by design — never probabilistic guesswork.

A compact, non-neural algorithmic pipeline runs entirely on local silicon. Exact, predictable output. Zero internet lag. Industrial immunity to glare, dust and movement.

SSV

Spatiotemporal Signal Validation

Analyzes pixel clusters across a rolling multi-frame window, filtering transient anomalies — light flashes, dust, background pedestrians — for extreme noise immunity.

KAL

Predictive Kalman Filtering

Historical trajectory vectors predict the hand's next coordinate, holding a rock-steady marker through momentary sensor disruption or natural hand tremor.

HMG

Homography Matrix Estimation

Projective geometry corrects perspective skew, so a surface mapped from an extreme ceiling or floor angle still tracks point-of-intent with pixel accuracy.

RAM

Innate Privacy Compliance

Spatial tracking happens frame-by-frame entirely in local volatile memory. No video is saved, cached or transmitted — privacy by architecture, not policy.

SurfaceWare Central · client portal

Zero-trust, headless onboarding.

No shared credentials, no command line, no physical hooks. Every device provisions itself through a short-lived, out-of-band token.

STEP 01

Device boot

The ARM/x86 unit installs a temporary cryptographic state on power-on.

STEP 02

Auto-request

It contacts the registry via gRPC and exposes a 6-character out-of-band token.

STEP 03

Portal validation

An authorized operator validates the token at central.hoverse.ai.

STEP 04

Key issuance

A unique, revocable API key writes directly to secure local storage.

STEP 05

Runtime pull

Central controls sensitivity, experience URL and timeout. Licenses are per-device and enforced live.
