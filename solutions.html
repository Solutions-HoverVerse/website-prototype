Retail · Hover SR™ (Sentient Retail)

Convert passive environments into intent-driven transaction engines.

Give the shop floor the analytics of an online store.

Online stores track every hover, click and abandoned cart. Physical stores see only who walks in and what rings up. Hover SR turns the store into a live, interactive web page — capturing the "invisible middle" of the in-store journey and translating real-world behavior into standard e-commerce funnel analytics. Anonymously.

Application 01 · Sentient analytics

E-commerce precision, offline

Measure physical click-through, product consideration time and in-aisle cart abandonment — then trigger real-time, on-shelf cross-sell.

Anonymous & compliant

Reads abstract radio-wave and volumetric signals — no facial recognition, no MAC tracking. Safe under GDPR and CCPA.

Application 02 · Touchless interactive commerce

Beyond measurement, Hover SR makes the glass itself interactive. A compact, outward-facing RealSense depth camera converts existing displays, video walls and street-facing windows into contact-free "aim, hold, commit" checkout engines — no touchscreen, no app download, no friction.

Legacy display conversion

The outward-facing RealSense depth camera retrofits onto existing non-interactive displays and video walls — turning them into fully interactive touchless nodes with no new terminal hardware.

Daylight-robust through glass

Calibrated to hold tracking accuracy through thick storefront glass and under harsh, direct solar glare.

24/7 off-hours commercialization

Safely turns external-facing windows into secure, interactive shopping nodes around the clock — no truck rolls to deploy.

Healthcare · Hover Health™

Enforcing the hospital shield at critical patient touchpoints.

Touchless clinical workstations that break a pathogen vector.

Shared keyboards, mice and touchscreens on Workstations on Wheels (WOWs) are touched dozens of times per shift and travel room to room — a documented mobile fomite shown to harbor MRSA, VRE and C. difficile. Hover Health replaces them with a voice-plus-gesture interface on Hover OS: remove the touched surface, remove a cross-contamination vector that drives healthcare-associated infections.

WOW

Low-CAPEX retrofit, highest-risk surface first

An outward-facing sensor retrofits onto existing carts, diagnostic screens and nurse-station monitors — starting with the Workstation on Wheels, the prime cross-contamination vector — with no expensive display overhaul.

EHR

An overlay on the records you already run

Works over Epic, Oracle Health/Cerner and MEDITECH — covering the vast majority of patient beds — and a big, fast, easy UI cuts the click burden behind clinician burnout.

MM

Multimodal by design

Kinetic inference and spatiotemporal tokens fuse voice with low-latency skeletal gesture — so charts scroll and zoom without speaking PHI aloud.

Burn recovery & physical therapy

Rehabilitation that never has to touch healing skin.

Recovery depends on precise, longitudinal tracking of range of motion and limb extension — but goniometers and strap-on wearables add friction and risk tearing grafts on hyper-sensitive skin. HoverVerse turns the therapy room into a contactless, data-rich diagnostic space: deterministic depth-vision tracks skeletal movement with no markers, no wearables and no contact, while gamified targets keep patients engaged.

100% contactless tracking

High-resolution skeletal and joint-angle telemetry with zero patient-worn markers or skin tethers — nothing touches healing tissue or grafts.

Upper & lower limb, two axes

A horizontal wall surface gamifies upper-limb reaching; an overhead downward camera performs contactless goniometry on strides, leg extensions and gait clearance.

Automated, longitudinal data

Every session logs precise coordinates; SurfaceWare Central aggregates anonymized progress and maps it into EHR analytics (e.g. Cerner PowerChart) over standard web APIs.

Biosafety labs & cleanrooms

In a sterile zone, a single touch breaks the barrier.

In BSL-3/BSL-4 labs and ISO Class 1–9 cleanrooms, keyboards, mice and touchscreens break air barriers, strain heavily-suited personnel, and act as persistent vectors for cross-contamination and particulate ingress. HoverVerse replaces them with touchless, multimodal control — gestures tracked through the containment sash, the UI short-throw-projected onto the workbench or rear wall. Fully offline at the edge, LIMS-integrated and validation-ready.

Protects the laminar air curtain

No reaching out of the safety cabinet for a mouse. Hands are tracked through the glass sash with reliable command recognition, so the air barrier is never broken.

Ends the disposable barrier economy

No plastic keyboard sleeves or mouse wraps, no doffing gloves to type at an external terminal — reclaiming the hands-on-bench time that gowning and barrier friction otherwise destroy.

Validation-ready & fully offline

Cleanroom-safe cameras in chemically inert housings, a projected interface with no touchscreen to contaminate, and all processing done locally — results feed securely into your lab system. Designed to meet FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and ISO/IEC 17025.
